NOT CURRENTLY SET UP FOR LOL GM

Using a LOL GM account currently only enables one feature: it stores your achievements in the cloud. This way, you can have leagues in different browsers and different computers that all contribute to your achievements. If that doesn't sound compelling to you, you don't have to make an account and you can still view your achievements from leagues in this browser. But hopefully more exciting features will be coming in the future!
